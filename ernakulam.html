ERNAKULAM

Ernakulam District was formed on 1 April 1958. The word/name Ernakulam is derived from a Tamil word Erayanarkulam which means abode of Lord Shiva. Ernakulam district is situated almost at the middle of Kerala State and on the coast of the Arabian Sea. It is also the most industrially advanced and flourishing District of Kerala.

Ernakulam is a good shopping centre and the seat of the district head quarters and the state's High Court. Ernakulam District is situated almost at the middle of Kerala State and on the coast of the Arabian Sea. Kochi is known as the 'Queen of Arabian Sea'. The first Stock Exchange of Kerala was started at Cochin in 1978. Thripunithura Hill Palace is the largest Archaeological Museum in Kerala. In 1990 February Ernakulam district become the first fully Literate district in India.

Ernakulam district also hosts the highest number of international and domestic tourists in Kerala state. This district was formed in 1958 by carving out regions from Thrissur and Kottayam district.The district is bounded by a 46.2 km. coastline of the Arabian Sea on the west.

Top 5 Destinations



Bharanikuzhi waterfalls

Bharanikuzhi is a small seasonal waterfalls that appears magically in a rocky stream originating from the mountains and hills of Malayattoor forest division. It's an eco-destination situated in the backdrops of malayattoor hills. This a beautiful eco-destination with sizzling waters that will steal the heart of any traveler.

Kodanad

Kodanad lies on the southern bank of Periyar River amongst the scenic beauty of the high ranges. It is 45 km from the city of Kochi and 12 km to the east of Perumbavoor town. There is an elephant taming centre here and elephant rides are provided here. There is also a mini zoo at Kodanadu.

Willingdon Island

This is a man-made island created from the earth dredged, while deepening the Kochi port. Lord Bristow was the architect of this island. There are frequent ferry and bus services from Ernakulam. The harbour, naval airport and harbour railway terminus are in this island.

Fort Kochi Beach

Fort Cochin beach in Cochin is one of the most famous beach of Kerala and is a pride of South India. The beach is very neat with tranquil atmosphere and attracts thousands of tourist every year.Located amidst coconut palm grooves and softly lashing seawater favorite place for tourist in Cochin. The beach is also famous for Chinese fishing nets which can be seen all along the coastline.

Hill Palace Museum

This is situated 12 km from Ernakulam on the Ernakulam-Chottanikkara route. It is the largest archaeological museum of Kerala. Different types of sculptures, collection of rare coins, old weapons and collections from the Cochin royal family are exhibited here.
